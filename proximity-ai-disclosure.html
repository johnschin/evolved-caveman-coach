5. AI Limitations — What You Need to Know

AI-generated responses can and do contain errors. Before relying on any information provided by an AI Coach, you should understand the following:

  • Hallucinations: AI models can generate responses that are factually incorrect, including fabricated citations, invented statistics, or inaccurate summaries of research;
  • No knowledge of your full situation: The AI Coach only knows what you share within the current session. It cannot account for your full personal history, health, medical records, family background, legal situation, or other context it has not been told;
  • Not personalized clinical advice: AI-generated coaching suggestions are general in nature and may not be appropriate for your specific circumstances;
  • Potential for inappropriate responses: Despite safety filters, the AI Coach may occasionally produce responses that are incomplete, irrelevant, or not suited to your situation; and
  • No continuity across sessions: The AI Coach does not remember previous conversations. Each session starts fresh.

Always use your own judgment. For important decisions involving your health, mental health, relationships, finances, employment, or legal matters, consult a qualified professional.

6. Safety Protocols and Crisis Response

The AI Coaches are equipped with automated safety protocols designed to recognize certain crisis-related language, including statements that may suggest suicidal ideation or self-harm intent. When these protocols are triggered, an AI Coach will:

  • Stop the current coaching conversation;
  • Acknowledge what you shared in a non-judgmental way;
  • Clearly state the limits of the AI Coach as a non-clinical, non-crisis tool; and
  • Provide relevant crisis resources.
Crisis and Emergency Resources
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (US)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
  • Emergency Services: Call 911

These safety protocols are automated and imperfect. The AI Coach will not always recognize or appropriately respond to every crisis situation. Do not rely on any AI Coach to detect an emergency or keep you safe. If you or someone you know is in danger, call 911 immediately.
