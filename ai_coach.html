Before We Begin

What This Tool Is

The Inner Leadership AI Coach is an educational coaching tool designed to help you build self-awareness, emotional intelligence, and stronger communication skills. It uses the Inner Board Meeting model to help you lead yourself from the inside out.

What This Tool Is NOT

  • This is not therapy, counseling, or medical care
  • This is not a crisis service
  • This tool does not diagnose conditions or prescribe treatments
  • This tool is not a substitute for a licensed mental health professional

If You're in Crisis

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911

Your Privacy

Your conversations are logged to improve the coaching experience. No data is shared with third parties. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
