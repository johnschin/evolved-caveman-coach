What to expect

How Proxi He works

Proxi He is a conversational coaching tool built on the work of Dr. John Schinnerer — psychologist, executive coach, and men's development expert. It's designed for men who are functioning well on the outside but are wrestling with something on the inside: chronic stress, emotional reactivity, relationship friction, a growing sense of disconnection, or the quiet question of whether this is really it.

You bring a situation, a pattern, or a feeling you can't quite name. Proxi He will ask sharp questions, offer a reframe, and give you one concrete thing to try — not a lecture, not a list of affirmations. Think of it as a thinking partner who won't let you off the hook.

Core topics: stress and burnout, anger and emotional reactivity, relationships and communication, identity and meaning, self-leadership and decision-making.

Where to begin
