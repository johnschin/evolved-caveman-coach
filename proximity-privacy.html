5. AI Processing and Third-Party AI Providers

When you send a message to an AI Coach, your message is transmitted to Anthropic (the provider of the Claude AI model) for processing. Anthropic receives your message content as part of the API request. Anthropic's handling of data submitted through its API is governed by Anthropic's own privacy policy and terms; as of the date of this Policy, Anthropic does not use API request data to train its models by default.

When generating AI responses, the Products also use OpenAI's text-embedding API to match your message against a curated knowledge base. OpenAI receives your message content for the purpose of generating an embedding vector. OpenAI's handling of API data is governed by OpenAI's own policies.

You should be aware that messages you send to the AI Coaches are processed by these third-party AI providers and subject to their applicable policies.

7. Safety Event Data — Special Retention Rules

As described in the Terms of Service and required by California SB 243, when an AI Coach's crisis safety protocol is triggered, we log the event occurrence, timestamp, and safety response. This log does not contain your full conversation.

Safety event logs are retained for a minimum of three (3) years from the date of the event. They may be retained longer if related to a pending or threatened legal claim, regulatory inquiry, or as required by applicable law. Safety event logs may be included in anonymized, aggregated annual compliance reports required by SB 243.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Row-level security (RLS) policies in our Supabase database;
  • Magic link authentication (no passwords stored by the Company);
  • HTTP security headers on all web properties (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options);
  • Access controls limiting internal access to personal data on a need-to-know basis; and

No security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at John@GuideToSelf.com.
