Before We Begin

Welcome to the Journey Forward AI Coaching Companion, created by Joree Rose, LMFT. Before you begin, please read and acknowledge the following:

What This Is

This is an AI-powered coaching and personal development tool. It is designed to help you build self-awareness, explore your emotional patterns, develop communication skills, and practice mindfulness. It draws on Joree Rose's published work, courses, and coaching frameworks.

What This Is Not

  • This is not therapy, counseling, or mental health treatment.
  • This tool does not provide medical advice or diagnose conditions.
  • This is not a crisis service and cannot provide emergency support.
  • This tool is not a substitute for a licensed mental health professional.

If You Are in Crisis

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, thoughts of self-harm, or danger to yourself or others, please contact:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911

AI Disclosure

This coaching companion is powered by artificial intelligence. While it is grounded in Joree Rose's actual content and coaching frameworks, responses are generated by AI and may not always be perfectly accurate, appropriate, or complete. No AI system can replace the nuance, judgment, and relational depth of a human therapist or coach.

Your conversations are stored securely to provide coaching continuity between sessions. Conversations may be reviewed for safety monitoring purposes. Your data is not shared with third parties or used for advertising.
