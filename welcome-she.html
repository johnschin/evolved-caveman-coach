What to expect

How Proxi She works

Proxi She is a conversational coaching tool built on the work of Joree Rose, MA LMFT — mindfulness teacher, therapist, and author. It's designed for women who are ready to turn toward themselves: to build greater self-awareness, quiet the noise, and develop a more compassionate relationship with their own inner experience.

Whether you're navigating anxiety, chronic overwhelm, relationship patterns, a sense of emptiness, or simply the hunger for more presence in your own life — Proxi She meets you where you are. Sessions are warm, reflective, and grounded in mindfulness and positive psychology. You'll be asked questions that slow you down in a good way.

Core topics: emotional awareness and self-compassion, mindfulness and stress, anxiety and worry, relationships and boundaries, meaning, identity, and personal growth.

Where to begin

Start with how you're feeling right now — even if you can't name it

You don't need to arrive with a clear question. Sometimes the most useful starting point is just: "I'm not sure where to start, but here's where I am right now…" Proxi She will help you find the thread.

The more honestly you describe what's alive for you — even the messy, half-formed stuff — the faster the conversation becomes genuinely useful.
