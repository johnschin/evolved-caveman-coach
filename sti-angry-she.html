Overwhelm isn't a character flaw — it's a signal. These swaps help you honor what you're feeling without letting it run the room.

— Joree

Before you use these: If you're flooded right now, your nervous system is in protection mode — and no script will land the way you want. Place a hand on your chest, take one slow breath, and feel your feet on the floor. Even 60 seconds of that changes what's available to you. Then come back here.
