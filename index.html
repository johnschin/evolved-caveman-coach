Questions

What people ask before they start.

Is this a real coaching tool or just a chatbot?
These are purpose-built AI coaching tools trained on specific bodies of work — not generic AI assistants. Each coach has a distinct methodology, voice, and point of view. They respond the way John or Joree would: naming the pattern, asking one sharp question, offering a reframe, and giving you something concrete to do.
Is this therapy?
No. Proximity coaches are coaching and educational tools for self-leadership and personal development. They are not therapy, counseling, medical care, or crisis support, and are not substitutes for a licensed mental health professional. If you're in crisis, please call or text 988.
What's the difference between the three coaches?
Proxi He is built on John's 30 years of men's psychology and coaching. Proxi She is built on Joree's work as a therapist and mindfulness teacher. Proxi We is built by both of them for couples — focused on communication patterns, conflict, intimacy, and the work between sessions. Each has a distinct methodology, voice, and focus area.
Can I use this alongside therapy or coaching?
Yes — and many people find the combination more effective than either alone. The coaches are designed to hold the work between formal sessions: processing what came up, preparing for what's next, building the daily habits that make the larger work stick.
How does access work after I pay?
After purchase you'll receive a secure sign-in link by email. Click it to access your coach. No app download required — works in any browser on any device.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled at any time. Email John@guidetoself.com and we'll take care of it.
