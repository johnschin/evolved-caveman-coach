How Proxi We works

Proxi We is a conversational coaching tool built on the work of Dr. John Schinnerer and Joree Rose, MA LMFT — a psychologist and therapist who are also partners and co-coaches. It's designed for high-functioning couples who have built successful lives together but sense that their relationship isn't keeping pace — or for couples who are hitting the same walls and want to understand why.

You bring a pattern, a recent conflict, a frustration, or a question about your dynamic. Proxi We will help you see what's happening beneath the surface, offer a framework for understanding both of your responses, and suggest one concrete thing to try or explore together.

Core topics: communication and conflict patterns, emotional reactivity and repair, attachment and connection, intimacy and drift, rebuilding after distance or betrayal, relational leadership.

Note: Proxi We works best when each partner uses it as an individual thinking tool and then brings those insights into real conversations with each other — not as a replacement for those conversations.

Where to begin

Start with a recent moment of tension

Describe something specific that happened between you recently — not the whole history, just one moment. What happened, how did it escalate (or shut down), and how did you each feel afterward? The more concrete the example, the faster Proxi We can identify the underlying pattern.

You can also start from your own side: "Here's what I keep doing that I know isn't helping…" That's always a powerful entry point.
