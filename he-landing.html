What Proxi He is

A coaching conversation, available whenever you need one.

Proxi He is a conversational AI coaching tool trained on the frameworks, research, and methods of Dr. John Schinnerer — psychologist, executive coach, and author with over 30 years working with men. It's not a chatbot. It doesn't give generic advice. It responds the way John would: naming the pattern, asking one sharp question, offering a reframe, and giving you something concrete to act on.

Most men don't struggle because they lack information. They struggle because they can't see their own patterns clearly — and because the moment they most need perspective is rarely when a coaching appointment is scheduled. Proxi He exists for those moments. The late night. The morning before. The middle of a week when something's not right and you can't name it yet.

This is not therapy. It's not a substitute for professional mental health care. It's a coaching tool for self-leadership — designed to help you build the kind of internal clarity that makes everything else easier.

How it works

Three moves, every conversation.

1
You describe what's actually happening
Not the polished version. The real version — the irritability, the loop you can't get out of, the conversation that keeps not happening. The more honest you are, the more useful the coaching becomes.
2
Proxi He names the pattern, asks one question
Not a list of suggestions. Not a pep talk. A specific response: what's the underlying dynamic at work, what question cuts through it, what reframe shifts the angle, what one thing you can do next.
3
You build the habit of the daily rep
Change doesn't happen in a single conversation. It happens through consistent engagement with the patterns that run your life. Proxi He is where those reps happen — between sessions, before sessions, or instead of waiting until things get bad enough to force it.

What you'll work on

Four areas where men get stuck.

Stress, Burnout & Overload
Why successful men feel constantly stressed. Why "just relax" doesn't work. Burnout as a nervous system problem, not a character flaw.
Anger, Irritability & Reactivity
How men get stuck in anger without realizing it. Why small things set you off. Irritability as unprocessed stress, not a personality trait.
Relationships & Communication
Why conversations with your partner escalate so fast. Why logic stops working in conflict. How to say the thing without starting a fight.
Identity, Meaning & Midlife
Successful on the outside, unsatisfied on the inside. The quiet feeling that what you've built isn't quite enough. What comes next.
