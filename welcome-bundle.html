Where to begin

Start with the individual coaches before going to Proxi We

The most powerful sequence is to do some individual work first — Proxi He or Proxi She — and then bring that self-awareness into your couples work with Proxi We. Understanding your own patterns before examining the dynamic between you makes the couples coaching significantly more effective.

That said, if there's something urgent happening in your relationship right now, start there. Follow what's most alive.
