With My Kids

Kids don't need a perfect father. They need a present one. These swaps keep the connection open when things get hard.

Remember: The goal isn't to manage the behavior — it's to stay in relationship while the behavior gets addressed. Your regulated presence is the most powerful parenting tool you have.

15 swaps

❌ What he says
"Stop crying."
✅ Say this instead
"It's okay to feel that way. I'm right here."
❌ What he says
"Because I said so."
✅ Say this instead
"I need you to do this. I'll explain why when we're both calmer."
❌ What he says
"You're fine." (after a fall or upset)
✅ Say this instead
"That looked like it hurt. Are you okay?"
❌ What he says
"You're being ridiculous."
✅ Say this instead
"I can see you're really upset. What's going on?"
❌ What he says
"I don't have time for this right now."
✅ Say this instead
"I want to hear this. Give me five minutes and I'll come find you."
❌ What he says
"Man up." / "Don't be a baby."
✅ Say this instead
"Feeling scared is normal. You can feel it and still be brave — those aren't opposites."
❌ What he says
"Why can't you be more like your brother/sister?"
✅ Say this instead
"I see how hard you're working. Let's figure out what works for you."
❌ What he says
"You're so dramatic."
✅ Say this instead
"This feels really big to you right now. Tell me more."
❌ What he says
"Just ignore them." (about a bully or social conflict)
✅ Say this instead
"That sounds really hard. What happened? Walk me through it."
❌ What he says
"You need to toughen up."
✅ Say this instead
"Hard things are supposed to feel hard. You don't have to pretend otherwise with me."
❌ What he says
"That's not a big deal."
✅ Say this instead
"It feels like a big deal to you — tell me about it."
❌ What he says
"Stop being so sensitive."
✅ Say this instead
"You feel things deeply. That's not a problem — that's part of who you are."
❌ What he says
"You should know better."
✅ Say this instead
"That was a mistake. Let's talk about what happened and what to try next time."
❌ What he says
"We'll talk about this later." (and never follows through)
✅ Say this instead
"I want to talk about this tonight. Can we find 10 minutes before bed?"
❌ What he says
"You're fine, it's not that bad." (minimizing pain)
✅ Say this instead
"I can see it feels bad. Do you want to tell me about it, or just have me sit with you?"
