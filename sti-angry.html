When I'm Angry / Reactive

Anger isn't the problem — it's information. These swaps help you use it without weaponizing it.

Important: If you're fully activated, none of these will work. Take 20 minutes first — your cortisol needs time to clear. Then come back and use these. They're for when you're cooling down, not when you're erupting.
