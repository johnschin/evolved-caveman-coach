With My Partner

The goal isn't to be right — it's to stay connected while being honest. These swaps invite understanding rather than defensiveness. They take practice. That's okay.

Before you use these: Notice where you are first. If your body is tense, your breath is short, or you feel that rising heat — pause. Even one slow, conscious breath creates a little space between the feeling and the words. These land so much differently when they come from a grounded, open-hearted place.
