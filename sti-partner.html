With My Partner

The goal isn't to win — it's to stay connected while being honest. These swaps won't always feel natural at first. That's the point.

Before you use these: The words matter less than your tone and intent. If you're still activated, take a breath first. A regulated nervous system makes everything below land better.

20 swaps

❌ What he says
"You're overreacting."
✅ Say this instead
"I can see this really matters to you. Help me understand what's going on."
❌ What he says
"I was just joking."
✅ Say this instead
"That came out wrong. I didn't mean to hurt you."
❌ What he says
"Here's what you should do…" (unsolicited advice)
✅ Say this instead
"What would feel most helpful right now — advice or just being heard?"
❌ What he says
"Fine. Whatever."
✅ Say this instead
"I need a few minutes. Can we come back to this in 20?"
❌ What he says
"You always do this."
✅ Say this instead
"When this happens, I feel disconnected. I want us to figure it out."
❌ What he says
"I already said I was sorry. What more do you want?"
✅ Say this instead
"I know my apology doesn't fix it yet. What would actually help right now?"
❌ What he says
"You're being too sensitive."
✅ Say this instead
"That landed harder than I intended. That's on me."
❌ What he says
"Why do we always have to talk about this?"
✅ Say this instead
"This matters to you, so it matters to me. I'm listening."
❌ What he says
"I'm fine." (when clearly not fine)
✅ Say this instead
"I'm actually a little frustrated and still figuring out how to say it."

"You never appreciate what I do."
✅ Say this instead
"I'm feeling like my efforts aren't landing. Can we talk about that?"

✅ Say this instead

"That's not what I said."
✅ Say this instead
"I think I came across differently than I meant to. Let me try again."

"Can't we just drop it?"
